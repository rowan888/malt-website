About

A cosy & characterful
bar.

Nestled in the seven arches of the Ropewalk, home to Maltby Street Market, MALT. is a cosy and characterful bar serving the finest UK craft beer, wine and cocktails.

Think candlelight, good company, and a carefully curated selection of drinks in the heart of Bermondsey.
